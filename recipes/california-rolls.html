Easy California Rolls

Description

Sushi seems like a daunting recipe to try. By traditional Japanese standards, this recipe may not suffice, but for the average hungry sushi-lover, this california roll recipe is the perfect easy snack! No crazy tools and simple ingredients, this recipe is perfect for a beginner!

Ingredients

Tools

  • Rice Paddle
  • Seran Wrap

Sushi Rice

  • 1 cup sushi rice
  • 1 cup water
  • Rice vinegar (to taste)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Sugar (to taste)

Fillings

  • Imitation Crab Sticks
  • Avocado
  • Nori

Steps

  1. Prepare rice first by cooking in a 1 to 1 ratio with water. If you don't have sushi rice, any short to medium grain rice will work.
  2. After rice is cooked, cool for 20 min. This is very important to ensure that your rice has the correct texture and it not too mushy later on!
  3. Add rice vinegar, salt, and sugar to rice and mix into rice with rice paddle.
  4. Cut avocado into thin strips
  5. Cut nori down to size and place on seran wrap. Press rice down all over nori
  6. Add imitation crab and avocado
  7. Roll up sushi with seran wrap. Do not press down too much, just try to get a cylinder like shape. Seal the roll by "gluing" the nori with water.
  8. Take off the seran wrap and cut into pieces with a sharp knife.
  9. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and ginger and enjoy!
