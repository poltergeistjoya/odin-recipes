Gochuchang Smashed Potatoes

Description

These gochujang smashed potatoes are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. Classic smashed potatoes with a sweet and spicy korean twist! Perfect as a party favor or finger food!

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 1 tbs gochujang
  • 1/2 Lime juice
  • 2 tsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tbs butter

Potatoes

  • 10 fingerling potatoes
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Scallion

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until soft, about 8 minutes. Don't boil for too long or they will fall apart.
  2. Let the potatoes cool for 10 minutes.
  3. While potatoes are cooling, mix together all the ingredients for marinade in a bowl.
  4. Smash potatoes with a mug after they have cooled down. If the potatoes have not cooled enough, they will fall apart when smashed.
  5. Heat oil up in pan and fry both sides of potatoes until crispy.
  6. Once both sides of the potato are crispy, add the marinade to the pan.
  7. Cook for another minute, flip with spatula if desired and then remove from heat.
  8. Top with chopped scallion and enjoy!
